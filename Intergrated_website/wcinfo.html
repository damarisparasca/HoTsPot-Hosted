How does the calculator work?




The WaterCredits and nitrogen emissions calculator is designed to help farmers assess their current management and its impact on water quality. It compares the Government's set nitrogen emissions with the farmer's actual emissions and assigns WaterCredits based on this.

Its functionality is based on the INITIATOR model developed by De Vries, Kros, Oenema, and De Klein. The calculations used to obtain the output are displayed to the right.

These calculations allow us to calculate nitrogen emissions on a farm-level. What WaterCredits do is compare this farm-level emissions to a Government set, based on EU policy, nitrogen farm-level threshold. The amount of kgs over oe under the threshold will determine the WaterCredits assigned. The thresholds are extracted from RVO and change based on farm characteristics (in kg N/ha/year):

  • Housing and Storage of Nitrogen Emissions
    • Emissions from housing: Nem,h = frNEm,h × Nin,pr
    • Nitrogen input to housing system: Nin,pr = Nin,am - Nem,h
  • Ammonia Emissions from Grazing and Fertiliser
    • NH3em,a = frNH3em,a × Nin,am + frNH3em,g × Nin,ag
  • Nitrogen Uptake
    • Nup = Nup,min + frup × (Nin,eff - Nem,a)
  • Nitrification and Denitrification in Soil
    • Nitrification: Nni,s = frni,s × (Nin - Nem,a - Nup - Nim,s)
    • Denitrification: Nde,s = frdes × Nni,s
  • Leaching into Groundwater
    • Ammonium leaching: NH₄gw = (1 - frRo) × NH₄ex
    • Nitrate leaching: NO₃gw = (1 - frRo) × NO₃ex
  • Runoff to Surface Water
    • Nitrate runoff: NO₃ro,sw = frro × NO₃ex
    • Ammonium runoff: NH₄ro,sw = frnro × NH₄ex
    • Total runoff to surface water: Nro,sw = NO₃ro,sw + NH₄ro,sw
  • Runoff to Sea
    • Nsea = (1 - frret) × (Nif + Ndep)
